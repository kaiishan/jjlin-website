About me

JJ Lin

Wayne Lim Junjie better known by his stage name JJ Lin, is a Singaporean singer, songwriter, record producer, and actor. Lin has received three Golden Melody Awards, including one Best New Artist and two Best Mandarin Male Singer.

He is well known for his songwriting and singing skills. He has written songs for various musical artists while he was still a trainee under Ocean Butterflies.Now, he has written over a hundred songs for other artists. His notable compositions include "Remember" for Taiwanese singer A-Mei, Harlem Yu's "What's Wrong With You?", Cyndi Wang's "When You".
